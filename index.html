THE RETAIL CHALLENGE

Over 20% of Sales are Falling Through the Cracks

Shoppers expect retailers to guide, solve, and instill confidence. Most simply display products and hope for the best. Here’s where it falls apart:

No Guidance at Entry

Shoppers walk in and often don’t know where to start. There’s no clear path, no visible support, and no easy way to ask for help.

Too Many Choices, too little information

Endless options with no context. Shoppers don’t know what fits their needs or what sets products apart.

Missing Product Information

Shoppers have specific, situational questions—“Will this work for me?” “What’s the difference?” The shelf doesn’t explain, and staff often can’t.

Questions Left Unasked

Many shoppers hold back—they don’t want to seem uninformed, bother staff, or invite a sales pitch. So their questions go unanswered.

No Insight Into Buyer Behavior

When someone leaves without buying, there's no way to know what went wrong or what they needed.
